Lilliputian

 (lil-i-PYOO-shuhn) adjective

   Very small.

noun

   A very small person.

[After Lilliput, a fictional island nation in Jonathan Swift's satirical
novel Gulliver's Travels. Everything was diminutive in Lilliput -- its
inhabitants were six inches in height.]
