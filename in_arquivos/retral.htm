Retral

PRONUNCIATION:
(REE-truhl, RE-) 
 
MEANING:
adjective: 1. Located at the back. 2. Backward.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin retro (back). Earliest documented use: 1822.
 
USAGE:
“Donley and Lindsay worked their way to retral chairs furthest from the speaker.”
J.M. Barlog; Necessary Measures; BAK Books; 1997.
