Effete (i-FEET) adjective

 

   1. Worn out; no longer fertile or productive.

   2. Weak, ineffectual.

   3. Marked by decadence or self-indulgence.

   4. Effeminate.

 

[From Latin effetus (worn out from bearing), from ex- + fetus (bearing young).]
