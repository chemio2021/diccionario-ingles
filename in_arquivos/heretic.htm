Heretic (HER-i-tik) noun

 

   One who holds unorthodox or unconventional beliefs.

 

adjective

 

   Not conforming to established beliefs.

 

[From Middle English heretik, from Middle French heretique, from Late Latin

haereticus, from Greek hairetikos (able to choose), from haireisthai (to choose).]
