Sulfurous or sulphurous

PRONUNCIATION:
(SUHL-fuhr-uhs, suhl-FYOOR-uhs) 
 
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Relating to or resembling sulfur.
2. Pale yellow.
3. Fiery; hellish.
4. Hot-tempered.
5. Profane, blasphemous.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin sulfur. Earliest documented use: 1530.
