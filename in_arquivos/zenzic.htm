Zenzic

PRONUNCIATION:
(ZEN-zik) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Square of a number.
adjective: Relating to the square of a number.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin zensus, alteration of census (square power), a specific sense development of census (a registration of Roman citizens and their property). Earliest documented use: 1557.
