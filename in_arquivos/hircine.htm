Hircine

PRONUNCIATION:
(HUHR-syn, -sin)
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Of or relating to a goat.
2. Having a strong odor.
3. Lustful; lewd.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin hircus (goat). Earliest documented use: 1656.
 
USAGE:
