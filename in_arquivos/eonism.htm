Eonism (EE-uh-niz-uhm) noun

   Adoption of female clothing and manners by a male.

[After Chevalier d'Éon (1728-1810), a spy and soldier
who lived the second half of his life as a woman.]

-Anu Garg (garg wordsmith.org)

  "Major Fosdick has converted a not uncommon introversion into eonism."
   Robert K. Morris; The Novels of Anthony Powell; University of Pittsburgh
   Press; 1968.
