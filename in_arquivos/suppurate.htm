Suppurate (SUHP-yuh-rayt) verb intr.

 

   To produce or secrete pus.

 

[From Latin suppuratus, past participle of suppurare, from sub- + pur- (pus).]

 

  "From one perspective, a certain irony attends the publication of any

   good new book on American usage. It is that the people who are going to

   be interested in such a book are also the people who are least going to

   need it. ... The sorts of people who feel that special blend of wincing

   despair and sneering superiority when they see EXPRESS LANE - 10 ITEMS

   David Foster Wallace; Tense Present: Democracy, English, And the Wars Over
