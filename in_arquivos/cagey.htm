Cagey

PRONUNCIATION:
(KAY-jee)
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Evasive; reticent.
2. Shrewd; crafty.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
Origin unknown. Perhaps from cage, from cavea (birdcage), from cavus (hollow).
 
USAGE:
"NBC execs were cagey about the prospects of Paula Abdul landing on one of the network's reality-competition programs."
Alex Strachan; More Reality Shows for NBC and Maybe Paula Abdul; The Gazette (Montreal, Canada); Aug 6, 2009.
