Spendthrift (SPEND-thrift) noun

 

   One who spends money extravagantly or wastefully.

 

adjective

 

    Wasteful with money.

 

[A spendthrift is literally one who spends his or her wealth, from Middle English thrift (prosperity), from Old Norse thrifask (thrive).]

 

Two colorful synonyms of today's word are dingthrift and scattergood.
