Sabulous (SAB-yuh-luhs) adjective

 

   Sandy; gritty.

 

[From Latin sabulum (sand).]

 

  "Clinical disorders of the lower urinary tract of cats are not new

   phenomena. Kirk, for example, described `retention of urine' as a

   cause of the problem was obstruction of the urethra by a sabulous

   material."

   Diseases in Cats, The Journal of Nutrition (Bethesda, Maryland), Dec 1998.
