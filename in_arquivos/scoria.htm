Scoria

PRONUNCIATION:
(SKOR-ee-uh) plural scoriae (SKOR-ee-ee)
MEANING:
noun:
1. In metallurgy, the refuse or slag left from smelting. 2. Porous cinderlike fragments of solidified lava.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin scoria, from Greek skoria (dross), from skor (dung).
