Graduand (GRAJ-oo-and) noun

   One who is about to graduate from a university.

[From Latin graduare (to graduate).]

-Anu Garg (garg wordsmith.org)

  "In order to add jollity to the proceedings, said the dean, each graduand
   would find beneath his seat a little tub of bubbles, complete with
   mortar board cap."
   Mike Amos; Proud Return of the Pigeon Boy; Northern Echo (Darlington, UK);
   Jul 14, 2005.
