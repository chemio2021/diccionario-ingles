Druthers (DRUTH-uhrz) noun
 
One's own way; preference.
 
[Plural of druther, contraction of "'d rather", as in "I/he/etc. would
rather ..."]

a) be able to get my innings over there that I would be able
to get here, and b) get the treatment I need."
Rob Bradford; Road Back Unfamiliar; Boston Globe; Mar 18, 2008.
