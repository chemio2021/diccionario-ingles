Calyculus or caliculus

PRONUNCIATION:
(kuh-LIK-yuh-luhs) 
 
MEANING:
noun: A cup-shaped structure.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek kalyx (cup, covering).
 
USAGE:
"It's attached over the whole inner surface of calyculus."
Queensland Naturalist (Australia); 1991.
