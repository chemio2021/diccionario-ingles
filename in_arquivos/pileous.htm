Pileous (PY-lee-uhs, PIL-ee-) adjective

 

   Covered with hair.

 

[From Latin pileus, from pilus (hair).]

 

  "What pileous sculptures was the young man creating on that lovely head?"

   Andrew Miller; Casanova in Love; Harvest Books; 2000.

 

  "A pileous alternative without slippage concerns is spray-on hair-in-a-can."

   Lyle Zapato; Aluminum Foil Deflector Beanie; Outer Limits Press; 2003.
