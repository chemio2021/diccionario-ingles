Garboil (GAHR-boil) noun
 
Confusion; turmoil.
 
[Via French and Italian from Latin bullire (to boil).]

to one increasingly confused victim."
Gerard Donovan; Dark Memories in the Forest; Irish Independent
(Dublin, Ireland); Jun 16, 2007.
