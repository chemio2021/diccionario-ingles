Fetial (FEE-shuhl) adjective, also fecial

 

   Relating to declarations of war and treaties of peace.

 

[From Latin fetialis, a member of the Roman college of priests,

who performed the rites in such matters.]

 

-Anu Garg (gargATwordsmith.org)

 

  "Joe -- in one of those fetial gestures by which we softened our

   warring -- had given me a couple of starts."

   O.A. Bushnell; Ka'A'Awa: A Novel about Hawaii in the 1850s;

   University of Hawaii Press; 1972.
