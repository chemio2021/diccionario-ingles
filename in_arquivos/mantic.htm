Mantic (MAN-tik) adjective

 

   Of or relating to divination.

 

[From Greek mantikos, from mantis (prophet), from mainesthai (to rage).

Ultimately from Indo-European root men- (to think) that is also the source

of words such as mind, mental, mention, Sanskrit mantra, automatic, mania,

money, praying mantis, monument, music, and amnesia.]
