Noisome

PRONUNCIATION:
(NOI-suhm)
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Offensive, especially to the sense of smell.
2. Harmful; noxious.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Middle English noy (short of annoy), via French, from Latin inodiare (to make hateful), from in- (intensive prefix) + odium (hate).
