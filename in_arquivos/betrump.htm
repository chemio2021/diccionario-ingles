Betrump

PRONUNCIATION:
(be-TRUHMP) 
 
MEANING:
verb tr.:
1. To deceive or cheat.
2. To elude.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From be- + French tromper (to deceive), which also gave us trumpery and trompe l’oeil. Earliest documented use: 1522.
