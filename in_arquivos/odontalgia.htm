Odontalgia

PRONUNCIATION:
(oh-don-TAL-juh -jee-uh) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Toothache.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek odont- (tooth) + -algia (pain). Earliest documented use: 1706.
 
USAGE:
“What’s his tag say, Hemingby? ...
Odontalgia. Treatment: Oil of Cloves?”
