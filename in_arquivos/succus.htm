Succus

PRONUNCIATION:
(SUHK-uhs) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Juice; fluid.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin succus (juice). Earliest documented use: 1771.
 
USAGE:
“For this reason, the extract and succus are usually prepared during the months of September and October.”
Hugh Chisholm; Encyclopedia Britannica; 1922.
