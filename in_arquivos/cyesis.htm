Cyesis

PRONUNCIATION:
(sy-EE-sis) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Pregnancy.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek kyesis (pregnancy).
 
USAGE:
“She greeted me at the door, her cyesis immediately evident, about the eighth month of it.”
Blossom Elfman; The Girls of Huntington House; Houghton Mifflin; 1972.
