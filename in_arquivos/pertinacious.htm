Pertinacious (pur-tin-AY-shuhs) adjective

   1. Holding resolutely to a purpose, belief, or opinion.

   2. Stubbornly unyielding.

[From Latin pertinac- pertinax, per- (thoroughly) + tenax (tenacious),
from tenere (to hold).]
