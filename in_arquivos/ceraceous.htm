Ceraceous

PRONUNCIATION:
(suh-RAY-shuhs) 
 
MEANING:
adjective: Waxy.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin cera (wax) + -aceous (resembling or relating to). Earliest documented use: 1768.
 
USAGE:
“The cloth ... was thickly coated with wax or some ceraceous substance.”
Seabury Quinn; The Dark Angel; Skyhorse; 2018.
