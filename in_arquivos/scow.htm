Scow

PRONUNCIATION:
(skou) 
 
MEANING:
noun: A flat-bottomed boat with square ends.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Dutch schouw (ferryboat). Earliest documented use: 1524.
 
USAGE:
“Small waves lapped over the scow edge.”
Jon Keller; Of Sea and Cloud; Tyrus Books; 2014.
