Plenary

PRONUNCIATION:
(PLEE-nuh-ree, PLEN-uh-ree)
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Full; complete; absolute.
2. Having all members of a meeting in attendance.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin plenarius (fully attended, complete), from plenus (full). Earliest documented use: 1425.
