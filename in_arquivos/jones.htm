Jones

PRONUNCIATION:
(jonz) 
 
MEANING:
noun: 1 One’s neighbors or social equals. Typically used in the phrase: keeping up with the Joneses.
noun: 2. An addiction or craving, especially for drugs.
verb intr.: To have an intense longing.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
For noun 1: From Jones, a common surname. The phrase was popularized by the comic strip Keeping up with the Joneses that ran in newspapers from 1913 to 1938.
For noun 2, verb: Of uncertain origin. Earliest documented use: 1965. Earliest documented use: 1879.
