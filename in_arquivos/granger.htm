Granger

PRONUNCIATION:
(GRAYN-juhr)
MEANING:
noun: A farmer.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
Via French from Latin granum (grain). Earliest documented use: around 1112.
 
USAGE:
"Megan glared. She touched Sena's hair like a granger examining blight."
Anthony Huso; The Last Page; Tor Books; 2010.
