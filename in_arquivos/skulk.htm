Skulk (skulk) verb intr.
 
   1. To hide, evade, or move stealthily.
 
noun
 
   1. Someone who lies in hiding, evades, or lurk.
   2. A pack of foxes.
 
[From Middle English skulken, of Scandinavian origin.]
