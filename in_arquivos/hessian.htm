Hessian (HESH-uhn) adjective
 
1. A mercenary soldier or a ruffian.
 
2. Burlap.
 
[After Hesse, a state in central Germany. Sense 1 derives from the fact
that Hessian mercenary served in the British army in America during the
American Revolution.]
