Idiolatry

PRONUNCIATION:
(i-di-OL-uh-tree) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Self worship.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek idio- (one’s own, personal) + -latry (worship). Earliest documented use: 1626. A synonym is autolatry.
 
USAGE:
“The idiolatry led to this ruin and the ruin of his race.”
Paul Collins; God’s New Man; Bloomsbury; 2005.
