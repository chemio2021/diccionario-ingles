Duodecimal (doo-uh-DES-uh-muhl, dyoo-) adjective

 

   Of or relating to the number twelve.

 

noun

 

   A twelfth.

 

[From Latin duodecimus (twelfth), from duodecim (twelve), from duo (two) +

decem (ten).]
