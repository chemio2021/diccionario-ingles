Salubrious (suh-LOO-bree-uhs) adjective

 

   Promoting health or well-being; wholesome.

 

[From Latin salubris, from salus (health), ultimately from Indo-European

root sol- (whole) which is also the source of words such as solid, soldier,

salutary, salute, salvage, safe, sage, solicit, solemn, and consolidate.]
