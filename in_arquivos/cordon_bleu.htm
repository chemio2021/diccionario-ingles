Cordon bleu (kawr don BLOO) adjective
 
Of the highest class.
 
noun
 
A person of great distinction in a field, especially applied to a chef.
 
[From French, literally, blue ribbon. Under the Bourbon kings in
France, a blue ribbon was worn by knights of the highest order.]
