Chaplet (CHAP-lit) noun

   1. A wreath or garland worn on the head.

   2. A string of beads.

[Middle English chapelet (wreath), from Old French, diminutive of chapel
hat, from Medieval Latin cappellus, from Late Latin cappa (cap).]
