Lowering (LOU-uhr-ing) adjective

   Sullen, frowning, gloomy.

[From Middle English louring, from louren (to frown, lurk).]

Today's word in Visual Thesaurus: http://visualthesaurus.com/?w1=lowering

-Anu Garg (words at wordsmith.org)

  "Miss Deane, with a lowering frown of her blackened eye-brows, found
   occasion to reprimand the elderly parlour-maid."
   John Davys Beresford; The Looking-Glass; 1922.
