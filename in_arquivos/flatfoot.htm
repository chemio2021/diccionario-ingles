Flatfoot (FLAT-foot) noun

 

   1. A condition of the foot in which the arch of the instep is flattened and the entire sole touches the ground.

   2. A police officer.

 

[Originally sense 2 refered to a foot soldier. In the past the term

has been applied to sailors, and to police officers who walked on patrol.

Today, it refers to any police officer and even to a detective.]
