Slaver (SLAV-vuhr, SLAY-vuhr) verb tr., intr.

   1. To slobber or drool; to smear with saliva.

   2. To fawn.

noun

   Saliva dripping from the mouth.

[Probably from Old Norse slafra (to slobber).]
