Liege

PRONUNCIATION:
(leej, leezh) 
MEANING:
noun:
1. A feudal lord.
2. A vassal or subject.

adjective:
1. Pertaining to the relationship between a feudal vassal and lord.
2. Loyal; faithful.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Old French lige, from Latin laetus (serf). Earliest documented use: 1297.
