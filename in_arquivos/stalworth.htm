Stalworth (STOL-wurth) adjective

   Stalwart: strong, dependable, firm.

[From Middle English, from Old English staelwierthe (serviceable),
from stathol (support) + weorth (worth).]

-Anu Garg (words at wordsmith.org)

  "That the theorists were on the money speaks well for the power of
   scientific speculation and calculation and is a testament to the
   stalworth pundits of the last half century who nurtured a field."
   Minas Kafatos and Andrew Michalitsianos; Supernova 1987A in the
   Large Magellanic Cloud; Cambridge University Press; Jun 9, 1988.
