Emanate or M-N-8

PRONUNCIATION:
(EM-uh-nayt)
MEANING:
verb tr., intr.: To emit or to come out.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin emanare (to flow out), from ex- (out of) + manare (to flow).
 
USAGE:
