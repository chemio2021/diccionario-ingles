Cocksure

PRONUNCIATION:
(KOK-shoor, kok-SHOOR) 
 
MEANING:
adjective: Arrogantly or presumptuously overconfident.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From cock (a euphemism for god) + sure, from Old French seur, from Latin securus (secure). Earliest documented use: 1520.
 
USAGE:
“Here, then, is the new mood in Brussels: confident but not cocksure.”
Mr Juncker’s Indian Summer; The Economist (London, UK); Sep 16, 2017.
