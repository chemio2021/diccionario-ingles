Denigrate (DEN-i-grayt) verb tr.

 

   To defame or belittle.

 

[From Latin denigratus, past participle of denigrare (to blacken),

from de- (completely) + nigrare (to make black), ultimately from

Indo-European root nek(w)t (night). Other words derived from the

same root are night, nocturnal, and equinox.]
