Forbearance (for-BAIR-uhns) noun
 
1. Refraining from enforcing something, such as a right or a debt.
 
2. Tolerance, patience, restraint, or leniency.
 
[From forbear, from Old English forberan (to endure), from for- (away) +
beran (to bear).]
