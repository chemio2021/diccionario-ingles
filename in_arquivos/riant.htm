Riant (RI-uhnt) adjective
 
Smiling, cheerful.
 
[From French riant, present participle of rire (to laugh), from Latin
ridere (to laugh).]

Sunshine, the eldest, with her riant smile, her radiant eyes ..."
Ouida; Randolph Gordon and Other Stories; Lippincott; 1867.
