Froufrou

PRONUNCIATION:
(FROO-froo) 
1. Something fancy, elaborate, and showy.
2. A rustling sound, as of a silk dress.

[From French, of imitative origin.]

MEANING:
noun:
1. Something fancy, elaborate, and showy.
2. A rustling sound, as of a silk dress.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From French, of imitative origin. Earliest documented use: 1870.
