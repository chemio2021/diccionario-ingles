Gung ho

PRONUNCIATION:
(GUHNG-HO) 
 
MEANING:
adjective: Extremely eager and enthusiastic.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Chinese gonghe, an acronym from the Gongye Hezuoshe (Chinese Industrial Cooperative Society). The term gonghe was interpreted to mean “work together” and was introduced as a training slogan by US Marine Corps officer Evans Carlson (1896-1947). Earliest documented use: 1942.
