Mediocracy

PRONUNCIATION:
(mee-dee-OK-ruh-see) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Rule by the mediocre.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
A blend of mediocre + -ocracy (rule). Earliest documented use: 1845.
 
USAGE:
“Why are gifted individuals always forced out by the mediocracy?”
Christopher Fowler; The Victoria Vanishes; Bantam; 2008.
