Labrose (LA-bros) adjective

 

   Having thick or large lips.

 

[From Latin labrosus, from labrum (lip). Other words derived from the same

Latin root are lip, labial, and labret (an ornament worn in a pierced lip).]

 

  "Maybe yu wouldn't mind talling us, my labrose lad ..."

   James Joyce; Finnegans Wake; 1939.
