Remontant (ri-MON-tant) adjective

 

   Blooming more often than once in a season.

 

[From French remontant, present participle of remonter (to remount).]

 

  "Like the flowers she has admired for so long in the pages of catalogs,

   Ella is remontant, `poised for a second season of bloom.' And she is

   surrounded by people who are cheering her on ...."

   Susan Larson, Flowerpower (book review: Ella in Bloom), The Times-Picayune

   (New Orleans, Louisiana), Jan 28, 2001.

 

  "Trent's Nursery in Christchurch has introduced a new series of bearded

   irises bred from the remontant Iris germanica."

   Bearded Irises are Top for Showiness, The Dominion (Wellington, New

   Zealand), Aug 18, 2001.
