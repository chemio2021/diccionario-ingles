Sutra (SOO-truh) noun

 

   A rule or formula; aphorism.

 

[From Sanskrit sutra (thread). Ultimately from Indo-European root syu- or su-

(to bind or sew) that is also the source of sew, suture, couture, Kamasutra,

and hymen.]
