Sic (sik) verb tr.

 

   To incite to attack, especially as a command to dog: "Sic 'em!"

 

[Variant of seek.]

 

adverb

 

   Thus; so. (Used after an incorrect or unusual word or

   phrase to indicate that it has been quoted verbatim.)

 

[From Latin sic.]
