Pollex (POL-eks) noun, plural pollices

 

   The thumb.

 

[From Latin.]

 

Hallux is the equivalent term for the big toe.

 

   "He caught his thumb between his gun and a sharp rock. It was a nasty

   cut spurting blood. I said, `Ben, that's it. We've got to get you to

   a doctor.'

   "`Heck no, let's get a turkey first,' answered Ben, quickly wrapping his

   dripping pollex with a handkerchief."

   Fred LeBrun, Just Another Turkey Shoot, The Times Union (Albany, NY)

   Nov 13, 1997.
