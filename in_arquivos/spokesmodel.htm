Spokesmodel

PRONUNCIATION:
(SPOKS-modl)
MEANING:
noun: A model who acts as a spokesperson for a product or organization.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
A blend of spokesman + model.
 
USAGE:
"It's just too fabulous that the former deputy prime minister of Canada, Sheila Copps, is the new spokesmodel for Herbal Magic diet products."
Deirdre McMurdy; My BFF Says; Ottawa Citizen (Canada); Sep 15, 2007.
