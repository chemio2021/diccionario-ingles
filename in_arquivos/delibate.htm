Delibate

PRONUNCIATION:
(DAY-luh-bayt) 
 
MEANING:
verb tr.: To take a small amount of something: to taste or sip.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin de- (from, away) + libare (to take a little of, to taste). Earliest documented use: 1623.
