Hermaphrodite

PRONUNCIATION:
(huhr-MAH-fruh-dyt) 
 
MEANING:
noun: 1. An animal or plant having both male and female reproductive organs.
  2. Someone or something having opposite qualities or characteristics.
adjective: 1. Having both male and female reproductive organs.
  2. Having opposite qualities or characteristics.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
After Hermaphroditus, a blend of Hermes + Aphroditus. Hermaphroditus was the son of Hermes (who also gave us hermeneuticand hermetic) and Aphrodite (who gave us aphrodisiac). Earliest documented use: 1400.
 
NOTES:
Nowadays, the term intersex is preferred over hermaphrodite when it’s applied to humans.
 
USAGE:
