Duck soup

PRONUNCIATION:
(DUHK soop) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Something that is very easy to do.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
Is duck soup very easy to make? How the term duck soup came to be known for an easily accomplished task is unclear. Earliest documented use: 1912.
