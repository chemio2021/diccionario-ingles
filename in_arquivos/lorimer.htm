Lorimer (LOR-i-muhr), also loriner, noun
 
   A maker of bits, spurs and other small metal accessories for horses.
 
[From Old French loremier, from Latin lorum (strap).]
 
-Anu Garg (gargATwordsmith.org)
 
  "The metal parts of saddles and bridles were made by the wealthy craft of
   lorimers."
   Abbott Payson Usher; An Introduction to the Industrial History of England;
   Houghton Mifflin; 1920.
