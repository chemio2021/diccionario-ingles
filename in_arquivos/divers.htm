Divers (DY-vuhrz) adjective

   Various; several.

[From Latin diversus, from divertere (to turn aside), from di-
(away, apart) + vertere (to turn). Ultimately from the Indo-European
root wer- (to turn or bend) that is also the source of words such as
wring, weird, writhe, worth, revert, and universe.]
