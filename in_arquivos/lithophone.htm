Lithophone

PRONUNCIATION:
(LITH-uh-fon) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Any of various musical instruments in which sound is produced by striking pieces of stone.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek litho- (stone) + -phone (sound). Earliest documented use: 1889.
 
NOTES:
